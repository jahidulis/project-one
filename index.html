Our Mission in 2022

Mission.

Our mission is to explore all 29 States of India within one year. That's our "Mission 2022". We will start our journey from 10th January & hopefully end it with 31st December. Our starting state will be West Bengal and then continue exploring throughout the heart of India. Eventually complete our mission in the newly made state of India which is also known as the " Heaven of Earth " Kashmir.

Vision.

The vision behind our journey is just to give our eyes and our soul to give a chance to consume all these unbearable beauty of our earth. We want to explore the deepest and undiscovered corner of this gigantic world.

Goals.

We are setting goal to travel the whole world. Though there is no time limit of this journey we pray that we will have the capability to keep travelling until our last day on this beautiful world.

Travel without limitations.

Everyone has the desire to travel but always there are some kind of limitation. These limitation are always so annoying and so much hassle that it destroys all the excitement of travelling. So in our mission we are going to overcome all this limitation and make traveling more easy, more comfortable.
